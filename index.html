Art 109 Three.js Project "View Less Explore More"

Click To Play

I wanted to create a big computer screen, and then use various icons I created, and then different sizes and different lens distances to make them the exact same size. The relationship between the idea of "world building", because now we are in front of the computer most of the time, or in front of the mobile phone. I want to use this to use people's eyes and create an illusion. There are many facts that we hide or do not want to see on the Internet, so this work serves as a warning, what we see may not be true, but we need to really understand and need to research and investigate by ourselves.
